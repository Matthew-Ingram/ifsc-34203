About

About

This site is intended to act as a form of documentation for various features of the HTML and CSS languages. It is intended to be used by both those who are looking for a resource to refresh their knowledge about the languages, and also by those who are just starting out with the languages. This site aims to communicate information primarily using visual and practical examples, and provides links to other sites for further readings and examples.
